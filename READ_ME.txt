Welcome to Mystery at Hawkshead Manor!

This is a choose-your-own-adventure game, where you play the role of a private detective. You will be presented with choices throughout the game as you endeavour to solve the mystery of who murdered Lord Hugo Hawkshead.

To play the game, run the game.py file. 

As you play through the game, you will receive points as you complete game actions. If you uncover lots of evidence whilst minimising the number of interviews you need to complete, you will get a higher score.

There are several endings...

Good luck!
